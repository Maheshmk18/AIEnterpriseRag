## üé§ Voice Recording Tips

### Tone & Style:
- **Professional but friendly**
- **Clear and confident**
- **Moderate pace** (not too fast, not too slow)
- **Enthusiastic** about the features

### Audio Quality:
- Use a good microphone (or headset mic)
- Record in a quiet room
- Speak 6-12 inches from the microphone
- Do a test recording first

### Delivery Tips:
- Smile while talking (it shows in your voice!)
- Take a breath between sections
- Re-record any section if you make a mistake
- Add pauses where indicated in the script
