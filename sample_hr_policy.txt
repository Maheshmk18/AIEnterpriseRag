Enterprise HR Policy Document
==============================

Leave Policy
------------
- Annual Leave: All employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year.
- Sick Leave: Employees can take up to 10 days of sick leave with a medical certificate.
- Maternity Leave: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
- Paternity Leave: Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave.

Work Hours
----------
- Standard work hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Flexible timing can be arranged with manager approval.
- Work from home is allowed 2 days per week.

Performance Review
------------------
- Performance reviews are conducted quarterly.
- Annual appraisals determine salary increments and promotions.
- All employees must complete self-assessment before the review.

Code of Conduct
---------------
- Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
- Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited.
- Company confidential information must not be shared externally.

Benefits
--------
- Health insurance coverage for employee and family.
- Life insurance with 3x annual salary coverage.
- Retirement benefits after 5 years of service.
- Annual bonus based on company and individual performance.
